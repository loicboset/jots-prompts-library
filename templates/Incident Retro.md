- What happened? (brief summary of the incident)
- When and how was the incident detected?
- What was the user impact?
- What systems, services, or components were affected?
- What was the root cause?
- What contributing factors made this worse?
- What went well during the response?
- What didn't go well during the response?
- How was the incident resolved?
- What immediate fixes were applied?
- What long-term actions should be taken to prevent recurrence?
- What signals or alerts should be improved?
- What documentation or runbooks should be updated?
- What did we learn from this incident?
- What follow-up tasks are required, and who owns them?
